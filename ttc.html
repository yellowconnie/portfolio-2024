freshie

freshie reimagines public transportation seating with a focus on hygiene, comfort, and personal space. Providing a cleaner and more comfortable experience for commuters.

RESEARCH
DESIGN
SKETCH
Team
Solo Project
Duration
November 2022

Always try to avoid the germs while riding public transportation?

The Toronto Transit Commission (TTC) has long dealt with challenges related to seat cleanliness, with reports of exposure to urine, feces, and vomit. More recently, concerns have also arisen regarding bed bugs.

Pain Points
Hygiene Concerns - Fabric seats easily absorb fluids and odors, creating significant hygiene issues for passengers.
Staining and Wear - Fabric seats are more prone to staining and wear out faster compared to harder materials, diminishing their appearance over time.
Exposure to Contaminants - There have been frequent reports of fabric seats being exposed to substances like urine, feces, and vomit, as well as concerns over bed bug infestations, further compromising cleanliness.
Design Intent

To enhance the ease of maintenance and ensure the cleanliness of public transport seats.

Enhance Public Transport Hygiene

Freshie aims to minimize the absorption of fluids and odors by utilizing easy-to-clean materials, reducing hygiene concerns commonly associated with fabric seats.

Promote Social Distance

By incorporating an open-wing design, Freshie helps to reduce accidental contact between passengers, promoting personal space and reducing the spread of contaminants in crowded public transport environments.

Increase Durability and Comfort

The design focuses on using materials that resist staining and wear, ensuring longer-lasting seats while maintaining passenger comfort through ergonomic design features like angled seating.

“Starting the day with a fresh and positive feeling!”

Open Wing Design:

The open-wing design naturally forms a physical barrier between passengers, clearly defining personal space.

the Less the Better:

Remove unnecessary area to reduce the proportion of bacteria on the surface.

Reduced Height - freshie is designed with an optimal height for the general public, offering a lower profile while still providing adequate back support.
Reduce the Use of Cloth - freshie uses minimal fabric to provide comfort and reduce the surface area for bacteria.
"Your life saver on public transportation."

Other Details

Slope the seat 8° from front to back & 15° back angle, to add more comfort.

Reflection

This project allowed me to further develop my technical drawing skills by hand, taking my proficiency to a new level. The detailed requirements of Freshie's design helped refine my precision and drawing ability, enabling me to push both my creative and technical boundaries.


Future Development
3D Modeling & Prototyping
Transform the current sketch into a detailed 3D model to better visualize proportions, ergonomics, and usability. This will allow for testing and refinement of the design in a more realistic form.
Material Selection & Optimization
Conduct further research into sustainable and durable materials that meet hygiene standards for public transportation. Focus on materials that resist staining, are easy to clean, and are comfortable for users.
Color & Aesthetic Refinement
Develop a comprehensive color palette and surface finishes to enhance the overall look and feel of Freshie. Detailed color research will ensure that the design is visually appealing while maintaining a sense of cleanliness and modernity.
