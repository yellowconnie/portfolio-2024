PunchMe

PunchMe is designed to offer a physical outlet for stress relief, allowing users to channel their frustration in a effective and engaging way. By incorporating responsive technology, the toy offers a fun and controlled method to manage stress.

Is traditional therapy not powerful enough for you?

In todayâ€™s fast-paced world, stress and anger management have become common challenges, with many people experiencing heightened irritability. While traditional therapy helps some individuals, many others do not find conventional methods effective in addressing their stress and anxiety.

Current Market

Does the current stress relief products on the market truly satisfy your need to manage stress?

Current stress relief products on the market, such as stress balls, fidget cubes, and squishy toys, often fall short in significantly reducing stress levels.

Too Cute

Many stress relief toys are overly cute and lack the intensity needed for aggressive stress relief.

Too Complicated

Some stress relief products require complex interactions, making them hard to use during moments of immediate stress.

Too Soft

Many toys fail to provide sufficient physical or emotional release, leaving the user still feeling stressed.

The Dangers of Improper Stress Relief

Engaging in aggressive or harmful ways to deal with stress can lead to unintended physical and emotional harm.


Typical examples include:

Yelling or raising their voice.
Hitting objects or slamming doors.
Punching walls or breaking things.

Easy & Straightforward

PunchMe offers a controlled and safe way to channel these aggressive impulses. Instead of destructive actions, users can relieve their stress by punching the toy, helping to reduce anxiety without the risk of damage.



JUST PUNCHME!

Instead of losing control during moments of high stress, the Punch Toy senses your frustration by detecting loud noises and encourages you to punch it to relieve your stress.

Design Process
Prototype Circuit

Ensuring compatibility between all components, from the Arduino board to the sound sensor, required careful wiring and testing to guarantee seamless functionality.

Write the Code

A simple task, like turning off the blinking LED after 15 seconds, took me considerable time to code. Multiple iterations were necessary to ensure proper timing and integration with other components.

Testing/Troubleshooting

Debugging both the hardware and software simultaneously added complexity, making this phase particularly challenging.

Enclosure & Sculptural Design

Crafting the silicone sculpture was costly and required precision. Balancing aesthetics with functionality, while managing production costs, was a major hurdle in achieving the final product.

Reflection

This project was both challenging and rewarding, as it was my first experience working with Arduino and C++. Learning to program and integrate the technology was a steep learning curve, but discovering the capabilities of Arduino ignited my excitement.

Despite the difficulties along the way, the sense of accomplishment I felt when I finally achieved the desired outcome made it all worth it. The process taught me resilience and problem-solving, and I was proud to see my vision come to life.
