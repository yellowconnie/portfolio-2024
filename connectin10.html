Connect in 10

Connect in 10 is a cardboard game for friends and family who want to bring back closer/stronger relationships and just have fun. Players come together for an exciting and fun-filled experience, reconnecting with their loved ones through this engaging game.

Users

The target audience for Connect in 10 is 4 to 5-year-old children and school-aged children, who are lacking social experience because of the pandemic.

What pandemic has affected this age group?
Limited opportunities to develop essential social skills.
These limitations contributed to feelings of isolation and loneliness.
Family members often couldn't provide companionship due to work and other responsibilities.
Design Intent

To encourages players to interact face-to-face, fostering deeper connections and creating a fun, engaging environment especially in the stressful pandemic period.

Project Concept



Flower Stem Patterns

Connect in 10! features flower-shaped patterns, inspired by how players twist and stretch during the game, intertwining their limbs like flower stems, symbolizing growing connections.

Geometric Shapes

The flower patterns in Connect in 10! are built from simple geometric shapes that naturally appeal to young children. Research shows that kids are drawn to these basic shapes, as they are among the first forms they learn to recognize, enhancing engagement and curiosity.

Reflection

This project was a great learning experience as it allowed me to explore mechanical and engineering aspects of design that I had no experience with.

I learned how to use Onshape to create 3D models and how to use mechanical engineering principles to design a well-functioning product.
